# Opening Sequence

## Context

This sequence is the soft opening before the constellation. It should be short and loving, centered on choosing Sophia, without asking her for a response.

Tone goals:

- Brief and clear.
- Loving, but not dramatic.
- No pressure to respond, forgive, or continue.
- Gentle enough that she can leave the experience without feeling cornered.

## Current Sequence Copy

1. Before the stars
   Sophia, I choose you.
   I know that more clearly than ever.

2. What I know
   No one comes close.
   Not in my heart. Not in the quiet places I keep coming back to.

3. What I want
   To protect your heart.
   Your peace, your softness, and the love I should have held with more care.

4. Still
   No pressure.
   You do not owe me a response. I just wanted you to feel loved here.

5. For you
   The stars are little moments.
   Each one is something I love, remember, or never stopped feeling.

## Editing Guidance

If you revise the copy, keep each line short. Avoid wording that asks for an outcome, such as "please forgive me", "come back", or "I need you".

Timing: each card currently lasts 6.3 seconds, with a gradual fade in and fade out plus a small fill bar at the bottom.
